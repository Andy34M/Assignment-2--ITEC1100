Pumpkin Spice is Peak

Blog post by Andra P

Personal Preference

I don't like coffee. I don't ever drink it. That does not make me underqualified when talking on the subject matter.

It's just not fall without pumpkin spice. The best sweet treats are made using it.

Here are a few examples of what you can make using it:

  • Pumpkin Pie
  • Pumpkin Cookies
  • Iced Pumpkin Spice Chai Latte

It has come to my attention that it is "cool" to hate on it, specifically lattes and chais. I am here to say that it is absolutely never cool to yuck someone's yum.

Other's Preferences

I have conducted a little experiment to see the basis of their feelings towards pumpkin spice. Here they are, ranked from most common reasoning to least:

  1. "I haven't actually tried it lol"
  2. "It's so overpriced"
  3. "I'm actually allergic to it"
